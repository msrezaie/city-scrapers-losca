*Except as otherwise indicated, meetings indicated herein begin at 9:00 a.m

HACLA has adopted a policy for Effective Communication with Individuals with Disabilities which is intended to ensure HACLA is able to effectively communicate with individuals with disabilities, including individuals with hearing, vision or speech impairments. A copy of the Policy is a available for viewing by clicking here.

Pursuant to the Policy, HACLA will furnish appropriate auxiliary aids and services, where necessary, to afford individuals with disabilities an equal opportunity to participate in the HACLA's public meetings, programs, services and/or activities. Examples of auxiliary aids and services include amplification headsets, sign language interpreters, note-takers, transcription services, written materials and large print materials.

To ensure availability, you are advised to make your request for an auxiliary aid or service at least 72 hours prior to the meeting/event you wish to attend. Due to the time required to secure certain auxiliary aids and services and/or identify alternatives when necessary, five or more business days' notice is strongly recommended. Requests should be directed to Tiffany Prescott at (213) 252-1826 or tiffany.prescott@hacla.org.
